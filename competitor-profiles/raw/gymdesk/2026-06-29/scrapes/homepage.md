## Value Proposition

**Three Core Pillars**:
1. **Take back your evenings from admin** — You didn't open a gym to spend your evenings chasing payments and updating spreadsheets. Gymdesk automates billing, tracking, and paperwork
2. **Bring in more members** — Word of mouth only gets you so far. Gymdesk captures new leads and follows up automatically with email and SMS
3. **Act on your numbers with confidence** — Gymdesk tracks your revenue, what each membership brings in, and how your gym is trending

**Brand Positioning**: "Less Admin. More Members. Zero Guesswork."

## Key Features (At a Glance)

- Memberships (unlimited plans, ranks, programs, family accounts)

- Attendance (kiosk, name search, barcode, phone)
- Booking (class schedule members book from phone)
