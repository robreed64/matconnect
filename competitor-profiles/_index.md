## Methodology

Each profile includes:
- **At a Glance** table with key metrics
- **Positioning & Messaging** (value prop, target audience, brand themes)
- **Product & Features** (capabilities, differentiators, integrations, roadmap signals)
- **Pricing** (structure, model, competitive positioning)
- **Customers & Social Proof** (named customers, industries, testimonials, reviews)
- **SEO & Content Strategy** (organic reach, content investments)
- **Strengths & Weaknesses** (objective assessment)
- **Competitive Implications** (where MatConnect is strong/weak, opportunities, threats)
- **Raw Data Sources** (links to underlying research)
