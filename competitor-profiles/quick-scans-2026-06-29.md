## 14. Mindbody (mindbody.com)

**Positioning**: "The default fitness studio software since 2001" — Market leader

**Pricing**: $139–$699+/month (published pricing varies; typical $250–$360/month). Real cost often 2x higher due to hidden fees:
- 20% commission on new client first purchase (capped $30)
- 3.5% per-transaction processing
- Training, customization, API fees ($0.002 per call)
- Real annual cost: ~$21,936/year

**Target Market**: Fitness studios, boutique fitness, gyms (enterprise segment)

**Key Features**: CRM, scheduling, POS, client management, automated marketing, lead management, AI front desk, 700+ partner solutions, exclusive marketplace for discovery

**Notable**: Market dominance through consumer-facing app (10-30% new client acquisition for studios). Major pain point: hidden fees and price increases.

## Key Insights from Quick Scans

### Pricing Landscape
- **Cheap entry**: DojoChamp ($99/mo) and Gymdesk ($75/mo) dominate price competition
- **Mid-market**: PushPress, Gymmaster ($89–$229), Glofox ($110–$199) target 50–300 members
- **Premium/Enterprise**: Hapana ($635+), Mariana Tek ($179–$285), Club Automation ($5K+), Mindbody ($500+ real cost)
- **Opaque**: Mindbody, Hapana, Momence, Jackrabbit hide pricing

### Market Segmentation
- **Martial Arts**: DojoChamp ($99), Spark Membership, Gymdesk ($75–$200), Jackrabbit (parent engagement)
- **CrossFit**: PushPress (free to $229)
- **Boutique Studios**: Glofox, Mariana Tek, Momence
- **Generic Gyms**: Gymmaster, Gymdesk, Mindbody, Club Automation
- **Enterprise**: Club Automation, Mindbody, Hapana

### Competitive Positioning Summary
- **Simplicity/SMB focus**: DojoChamp, Gymdesk, PushPress (free tier)
- **Enterprise/Multi-location**: Mindbody, Club Automation, Mariana Tek, Hapana
- **Boutique-focused**: Glofox, Momence, Mariana Tek
- **Vertical specialization**: DojoChamp (martial arts), PushPress (CrossFit), Jackrabbit (classes)
