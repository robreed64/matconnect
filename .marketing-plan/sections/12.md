## Summary

- **32 Acquisition ideas live in the plan** (primary stage for pre-revenue founder)
- **7 Activation ideas** (onboarding, email sequences, concierge)
- **7 Retention ideas** (triggers, win-back, support as marketing)
- **5 Referral ideas** (affiliate program, viral loops)
- **2 Revenue ideas** (upsells, price localization)
- **84 ideas skipped** — primarily paid channels (no budget), developer-specific tactics, and ideas requiring more social proof than year 1 has

**Interpretation:** The plan covers ~40% of the tactical surface area. That's appropriate for a solo founder bootstrapping to first revenue. The skipped 60% is the inventory for year 2 and beyond — as customers and budget unlock, these become the next layer of growth without needing to reinvent the strategy.
