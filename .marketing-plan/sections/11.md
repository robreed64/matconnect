# 11. Marketing Operations Stack

## The core principle

A solo founder + a lean toolset + the right skills can output the work of a 5-person marketing team if the tools are chosen for leverage, not comprehensiveness. Every tool on this list has to either save hours or enable something otherwise impossible.

## Current stack (bootstrapped, ~$50/mo)

| Tool | Stage | Purpose | Cost |
|------|-------|---------|------|
| Email provider (Resend) | Activation / Retention | Onboarding sequences, behavioral triggers, outreach | ~$0–20/mo |
| Plausible or GA4 | Measurement | Landing page traffic and source tracking | Free / $9/mo |
| YouTube | Acquisition | Demo video, tutorial library, SEO surface | Free |
| Loom | Activation / Acquisition | Async walkthroughs for prospects and new customers | Free tier |
| Facebook Groups | Acquisition | Community presence in BJJ owner groups | Free |
| Reddit | Acquisition | Founder story, community help on r/bjj | Free |
| Google Sheets | Referral / Measurement | Referral tracking, customer list, pipeline | Free |
| WhatsApp / iMessage | Retention | High-touch check-ins with early customers | Free |

**Total current spend: ~$20–30/mo.** Everything else is founder time.

## Stack additions by revenue milestone

### At 5 gyms (~$245 MRR)

| Add | Stage | Why now |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Notion or HubSpot Free (CRM) | Acquisition | 10+ active prospects is too many to track mentally |
| YouTube Studio analytics | Acquisition | Know which demo sections cause drop-off |

### At 10 gyms (~$490 MRR)

| Add | Stage | Why now |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Behavioral email triggers (Loops.so or upgrade Resend) | Retention | Manual check-ins don't scale past 10 customers |
| Loom Pro ($12/mo) | Activation | Unlimited length + viewer analytics — know if prospects watch |
| Simple referral link tool (ReferralHero free or promo codes) | Referral | Spreadsheet tracking breaks at 10+ active referrers |

### At 20 gyms (~$980 MRR)

| Add | Stage | Why now |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Google Search Console | Acquisition | Free — install now, data accumulates; don't wait |
| Ahrefs Starter ($29/mo) | Acquisition | Keyword data before writing any SEO content |
| Buffer or Publer ($6–15/mo) | Acquisition | Schedule Instagram posts without daily attention |
| Folk.app or HubSpot Starter | Acquisition | 20+ gym pipeline needs deal stages and follow-up reminders |

### At 30 gyms (~$1,470 MRR)

| Add | Stage | Why now |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Facebook Pixel on landing page | Acquisition | Needs 60 days of data before retargeting ads run |
| Meta Ads Manager | Acquisition | First retargeting test — $200–500/mo |
| Stripe Annual billing product | Revenue | Confirm annual billing is configured in Stripe/Square |

## Skills mapped to AARRR stages

| Stage | Skill | When to invoke |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| Acquisition — SEO | `ai-seo` / `seo-audit` | Q3, before writing any content |
| Acquisition — Content | `marketing-ideas` | Q2, when planning community content |
| Acquisition — Paid | `ads` / `ad-creative` | Q4, before first paid experiment |
| Activation — Email | `emails` / `onboarding` | Q1 week 3, building onboarding sequence |
| Retention — Lifecycle | `emails` | Q2, building behavioral trigger emails |
| Referral | `referrals` | Q1 week 9, building the referral program |
| Revenue — Pricing | `pricing` | Q2, after 5 paying customers |
| Launch moments | `launch` | Any time a major feature ships |

## What this stack replaces

| Traditional role | Replaced by |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Marketing coordinator | Resend sequences + Loom + Buffer |
| SEO specialist | `ai-seo` skill + Search Console + Ahrefs Starter |
| Paid media manager | `ads` skill + Meta Ads Manager (Q4) |
| Content writer | Founder's authentic voice + `marketing-ideas` for direction |
| Customer success | Founder direct + behavioral email triggers |

## What not to add

| Tool | Why not |
|------|---------|
| Salesforce / complex CRM | Overkill until 50+ active deals |
| HubSpot Marketing Hub | $800+/mo — not justified until $5K+ MRR |
| Intercom | Adds support load before the product is stable |
| Enterprise social tools | Buffer or manual posting is sufficient |
