# 1. Executive Summary

**MatConnect — 12-Month Marketing Plan**
*Bootstrapped · Pre-revenue · Solo founder · Goal: 3 gyms in 90 days → 30 gyms in 12 months*

## Three big bets

1. **Founder-led community sales** — The founder owns a BJJ gym and built this tool for themselves. That story is more credible than any ad. The first 10 customers will be won by showing up in the BJJ community as a practitioner who solved their own problem, not as a software vendor.

2. **Belt progression + kiosk as the hook** — No generic gym software has belt rank tracking with per-technique requirements or a true self-service kiosk flow. These two features are the wedge into every sales conversation. Everything else (billing, scheduling, marketing automation) is a reason to stay.

3. **Every customer is a distribution channel** — At this stage, word of mouth inside the BJJ community is faster and cheaper than any paid channel. The referral program gets built before month 3, before there's anyone to refer.

## 90-day priorities

1. Close the 1 warm prospect → first case study + testimonial
2. Direct outreach to 20 BJJ gym owners in founder's network → close 2 more
3. Ship a 3-minute demo video showing kiosk + belt progression in action at founder's gym
4. Lock in pricing and self-serve signup (so a gym can start without a call)
5. Launch a simple referral incentive for the first 3 customers

## 12-month outcome

| Quarter | Target gyms | MRR target | Key unlock |
|---------|-------------|------------|------------|
| Q1 | 3 | ~$150 | First 3 paying customers; case study |
| Q2 | 10 | ~$490 | Referral engine live; BJJ community presence |
| Q3 | 20 | ~$980 | SEO foundation; YouTube demo library |
| Q4 | 30 | ~$1,470 | First paid test ($200–$500/mo); partner gyms |

*MRR based on $49/mo Pro plan as primary SKU. Numbers are deliberately conservative — these are minimums, not ceilings.*
