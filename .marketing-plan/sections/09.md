# 9. 90-Day Roadmap

## Weeks 1–2: Unblock

| # | Move | AARRR | Owner | Done when |
|---|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | **Close warm prospect** — get them live, not just "interested" | Revenue | Rob | First invoice paid or Free tier active with kiosk working |
| 2 | **Install analytics** — Plausible or GA4 on the landing page | Measurement | Rob | Traffic source data showing in dashboard |
| 3 | **Film the demo video** — at your gym, on your tablet, unscripted, 3–5 min | Acquisition | Rob | Uploaded to YouTube (unlisted is fine) |
| 4 | **Fix the Academy tier** — remove or mark "coming soon" on landing page | Revenue | Rob | No misleading feature listed for a product that doesn't exist |

## Weeks 3–4: Foundation

| # | Move | AARRR | Owner | Done when |
|---|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| 5 | **Build the network outreach list** — 30 names of BJJ gym owners you know personally | Acquisition | Rob | List exists in a doc or spreadsheet |
| 6 | **Send first 10 outreach messages** — personal, not mass email | Acquisition | Rob | 10 messages sent, replies tracked |
| 7 | **Set up the 5-email onboarding sequence** — day 0/1/3/7/14 emails from §5 | Activation | Rob | Sequence live and triggering for new signups |
| 8 | **Add "Powered by MatConnect" to waiver confirmation emails** | Referral | Rob | Footer live in outgoing waiver emails |
| 9 | **Join 3 BJJ Facebook groups** — lurk and help, don't pitch yet | Acquisition | Rob | Member of groups, notifications on |

## Weeks 5–8: Velocity

| # | Move | AARRR | Owner | Done when |
|---|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| 10 | **Send remaining 20 network outreach messages** | Acquisition | Rob | All 30 contacts reached |
| 11 | **Close gyms 2 and 3** — from network outreach responses | Revenue | Rob | 2 more gyms live |
| 12 | **Get customer 1's testimonial** — add to landing page | Acquisition | Rob | Real quote live with name and gym |

| 16 | **Build the referral program** — referral code per customer, spreadsheet tracking, give-a-month/get-a-month | Referral | Rob | First 3 customers have their referral links |

## Weeks 9–12: Compound

| # | Move | AARRR | Owner | Done when |
|---|------|-------|-------|-----------|

| 19 | **Offer annual billing to monthly customers** | Revenue | Rob | At least 1 annual conversion |

| Not doing | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| SEO / content | Q3 — no bandwidth, no domain authority yet |
| Paid ads | No CAC data, no budget — Q4 at earliest |
| Podcast outreach | Need 5+ customers and a case study first |
| Product redesign | Out of scope for marketing plan |
| Hiring | Revenue needs to justify it first |
